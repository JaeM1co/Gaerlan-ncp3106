FAQs about Me

I chose Computer Engineering because it combines hardware and software, and I want to design and innovate technologies like computers, AI, and smart systems. It also gives me good career opportunities in a fast-growing field.

I'm interested in both hardware and software because I see how they work together, but I lean more towards hardware since I enjoy understanding how the actual components and systems function. At the same time, I’m currently more focused on networks because I find it exciting to learn how devices connect, communicate, and share information, which is a very important part of today’s technology.

After graduation, I plan to pursue a career in networking because I’m really interested in how systems connect and communicate. But I’m also open to a hardware-related career since I enjoy working with the physical side of technology as well.

So far, I’ve worked on projects that helped me apply what I’ve learned in class, like basic circuit designs, microcontroller-based systems, and some simple programming tasks. I’ve also done networking-related activities such as configuring routers and switches, which gave me hands-on experience in setting up and managing networks.
